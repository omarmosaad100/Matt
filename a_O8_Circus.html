A show that only kids love: The National Circus of Agouza

By Omar Mosaad

Clowns. What do you feel when you read this word? Probably horrified. Clowns get wide representation on horror movies, but in real life, they don't really make it to screens.

Adults look at clowns as if their are monsters who hide their vigilante creepy thoughts behind the make-up, while all they aim to do, is make kids laugh. In the Egyptian National Circus there are a lot of them, if you are interested to know their story, click here!

There are also some talented artists throwing some creative shows, we saw a man walking on thin wire, a man throwing daggers, and a young girl doing some insane moves.



We talked to this girl to further understand her work, we knew that she is a member of a family that all of them pursue the same job, most of whom are members of The National Circus. She gave us amazing insight, if you are interested to read more about the girl, click here!

Although the circus is loaded with such talents, it failed to attract enough audience. Maybe because it's easier for one with limited talent to shine, only if she or he has got social media skills. That's why we should take it in our hands to put the spotlight where it should be.
