Feel free to be different in.. Dahab

By Omar Mosaad



Ever wanted to wear something but felt you may be judged? Can't wear your style because it doesn't fit the culture? Okay, you may need to consider moving to Dahab. There, you can live as free as you want. There you can find men having piercings, women wearing bikini in the streets, and all hair styles possible, the good part is that no one cares! If you think you are different, believe me, you will fade out there.

But that's not the only good part, you will also have an everyday amazing night. The city never sleeps, and you have plenty of options when it comes to things you can do at night. You can go to the desert, build some fire, and chill. You can explore the city's restaurants and cafes, there are too many.

At morning, you can hit the beach, the sea is wonderful, such an amazing scenery, where you can see tons of fish. Just make sure you got the sea shoes with you, if you don't want returning with a bleeding feet. You can also climb a mountain, there is that site, where you go up a mountain for 30 mins, and reach a small lake where people jump in, and then go back. It may sound worthless, but believe me, it's such an experience! You can take a boat trip with your friends, visit some islands near the coast.

You definitely would need a job, there are many fields nourishing there, but mostly tourism and fishing. Tourism is a big field that gathers many fields around, you will definitely find yourself something to do.
