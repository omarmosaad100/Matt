Where everyone matters

We are a group of students at Arab Academy for science and technology, college of media and language, Smart Village Campus. We have created this website for our graduation project.

Its main idea is representing those underrepresented, giving voice to whom have been muted, and put the spotlight where it should be. During the last few years, media changed. Social media became more powerful, shifted somethings, margined some and highlighted some. But the problem is, that the algorithms followed by most social media platforms aim solely to gaining money, so it will for example, keep showing you similar content to make sure you like it, keep scrolling, and definitely keep seeing paid ads. When it would have been much better if they made sure you see different content, and in the same time, giving advantage for unique content, in this way everyone is seen. The good thing is, that's what we are trying to do here!

We first, intend to make it live forever by dedicating it to our college. We also have the vision of upgrading this website, we will seek more cooperation of users, and use as many eyes as possible, to make sure we are failing to kill some shadows!
